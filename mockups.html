Chrome contract — header & footer

Three scenarios for artisti7607.com modernization: same regions and counts; labels and destinations change.

Goal: Replace inconsistent, role-specific mega-menus with one predictable layout: logo, five primary destinations, four utility regions (FAQ, database search, language, session—session may include a compact role badge next to the account action). The fifth primary slot and the session region swap copy by audience; spacing and reading order stay fixed. Footer uses the same pattern everywhere: copyright plus four legal/contextual links.

Fifth primary: generic “Servizi” (or news hub). Session: Accedi.
